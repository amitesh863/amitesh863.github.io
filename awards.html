Pune Urban Data Exchange (PUDX) Datathon organised by Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems @ IISC Bangalore

Member of the winning team for Pollution Exposure Data Analytics.

Air Quality data, Rainfall data and location of the senors were given by the Robert Bosch Centre. We performed Seasonally Decomposed Missing Value Imputation using Kalman filter for missing time-series data and trained Neural Beats model for univariate forecasting of PM 2.5 values.
